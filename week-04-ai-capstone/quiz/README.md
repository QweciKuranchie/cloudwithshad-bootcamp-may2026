## 📖 How to take it

Open **[`Week4_Quiz_AI_Capstone.html`](./Week4_Quiz_AI_Capstone.html)** in your browser. No install, no internet needed.

As you answer questions correctly, a **live architecture diagram** builds on the right side of the page. Get all 10 right and you'll have built the full Week 4 architecture from scratch.

## 💡 Tips

- **Take your time** — there's no clock. Read each question twice.
- **The "Why this answer?" panel** explains every answer in detail. Use it.
- **Streaks count** — get 3+ in a row and the 🔥 emoji shows up
- **Retake it** — your first score is rarely your best. Try again after re-reading the slides.
