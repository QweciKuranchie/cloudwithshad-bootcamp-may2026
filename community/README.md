# 👥 Community

This is more than a bootcamp — it's a community. Here's how we operate.

---

## 📜 Community guidelines

**Be helpful.** Someone helped you when you were stuck. Pay it forward.

**Be honest.** "I don't know" is a perfectly good answer. Pretending wastes everyone's time.

**Be patient with beginners.** Everyone was new at some point. The person asking "stupid questions" today is the person hiring you in 5 years.

**Be kind.** West African tech is small. Reputation travels.
