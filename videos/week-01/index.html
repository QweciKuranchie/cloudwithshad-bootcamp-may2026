About this session

This is the live walkthrough from Saturday 23 May 2026 — the first session of the cohort. In two hours we covered everything you need to start building on AWS: how the cloud actually works, the three services that matter most on day one (S3, EC2, IAM), and a live demo where I deployed a portfolio site to S3 with CloudFront in front of it.

Watch this before starting Labs 1 and 2 — the labs will make significantly more sense after you've seen the live walkthrough.
