# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We're a community of learners, mentors, and friends-in-the-making. Everyone deserves to participate in a respectful, harassment-free environment — regardless of age, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, neurotype, ethnicity, nationality, education level, religion, or experience level.

## Our standards

**Things we love to see:**
- Patience with beginners — every expert was once a beginner
- Curiosity over judgment
- Sharing wins (yours and others')
- Helping someone get unstuck without making them feel bad
- Disagreeing with ideas, never with people

**Things we won't tolerate:**
- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
- Discriminatory jokes or language
- Doxxing or sharing private info without permission
- Spamming the WhatsApp group with crypto/MLM/job scam links
- Plagiarism (passing off someone else's project as your own)

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code:
1. Reach out to Shadrack directly on WhatsApp or LinkedIn
2. Or open a private message in the GitHub Discussions
3. All reports are confidential and taken seriously

Consequences range from a private warning to removal from the WhatsApp group and revocation of access to the cohort.
