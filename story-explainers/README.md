# 📖 Story Explainers — bonus learning

> **Cloud concepts explained through Ghanaian stories.** Single-page interactive walkthroughs that map AWS concepts to things you already know — markets, trotros, postmen, banking halls.

These aren't required to finish the bootcamp. But they're some of the most-shared content cloudwithshad publishes. Open one when you're tired of slides — let the analogies do the heavy lifting.

## 💡 How to use these

- **Stuck on a concept** during a lab? Open the matching story.
- **Studying for the Cloud Practitioner exam?** Open all six the week before — they make remembering effortless.
- **Sharing with a friend** who keeps asking "what's the cloud?". These were designed to be shareable. Send the link.

## ✍️ Have an idea for a new story?

We're always looking for great cultural analogies. Drop a suggestion in [Discussions](../../../discussions) or open a PR with the HTML.
